AMAZON INDIA SELLER POLICY DOCUMENT
Version: 2024.12

=============================================================
1. ACCOUNT HEALTH METRICS
=============================================================

1.1 Order Defect Rate (ODR)
Amazon requires sellers to maintain an Order Defect Rate below 1%.
ODR includes: negative feedback rate, A-to-Z guarantee claim rate, and chargeback rate.
ODR above 1% may result in account deactivation.

1.2 Pre-fulfillment Cancellation Rate
Must be below 2.5%. Rate is measured over a 7-day period.

1.3 Late Shipment Rate
Must be below 4%. Rate is measured over 10 and 30-day periods.

=============================================================
2. PRODUCT LISTING REQUIREMENTS
=============================================================

2.1 Images
Main image: pure white background, product must fill 85% of frame.
Minimum 6 images recommended for best placement.
Image dimensions: minimum 1000 pixels on the longest side.

2.2 Pricing
Sellers must honor the listed price. Price gouging during high-demand periods 
is prohibited and may result in immediate listing removal.

Reference Price Policy: Any crossed-out or Was price must be accurate and 
verifiable as the product's recent selling price.

2.3 Apparel Size Requirements
All clothing listings must include standardized size charts.
Measurements must be in centimeters (cm) for Indian marketplace.

=============================================================
3. FULFILLMENT AND SHIPPING
=============================================================

3.1 FBA vs FBM
Fulfillment by Amazon (FBA) sellers benefit from Prime badge and higher search ranking.
Fulfilled by Merchant (FBM) sellers must ship within the promised handling time.

3.2 Return Policy
Amazon's standard return window is 30 days from delivery date.
Electronics: 10-day return window.

=============================================================
4. APPEAL AND REINSTATEMENT
=============================================================

Appeals must include a detailed Plan of Action (POA) containing:
1. Root cause of the policy violation
2. Actions taken to resolve the issue
3. Steps to prevent future violations

Amazon Seller Performance team reviews appeals within 7 business days.
Incomplete POAs are returned for revision.
