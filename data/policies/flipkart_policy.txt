FLIPKART SELLER HUB POLICY DOCUMENT
Version: 2024.12

=============================================================
1. SELLER PERFORMANCE STANDARDS
=============================================================

1.1 Seller Score
Flipkart measures seller performance via a composite Seller Score (0-100).
Scores below 60 result in listing suppression.
Scores below 40 result in account suspension.

1.2 Return Rate Standards
Acceptable return rate: below 8% overall.
Category-specific thresholds apply for electronics (5%) and fashion (12%).

1.3 Ratings
Minimum acceptable product rating: 3.5 stars.
Sellers with average ratings below 3.0 will receive a performance notice.

=============================================================
2. LISTING POLICIES
=============================================================

2.1 Catalog Quality
All product listings must have accurate catalog data.
Incorrect category mapping may result in listing rejection.

2.2 Image Standards
Minimum 3 images per listing. Main image must be on white background.
Fashion and footwear require 5+ images including front, back, and detail shots.

2.3 Apparel Compliance
Size charts mandatory for all clothing and footwear.
Charts must include both Indian sizes and centimeter measurements.

=============================================================
3. PAYMENTS AND SETTLEMENTS
=============================================================

3.1 Payment Cycle
Flipkart settles payments every 7 days.
Payment reflects orders confirmed as delivered.

3.2 Deductions
Platform fee: 3-22% depending on category and fulfillment type.
Returns: Reverse logistics cost deducted from seller account.

3.3 Payment Disputes
Disputes must be raised within 45 days of settlement.
Use Seller Hub > Payments > Raise Dispute workflow.

=============================================================
4. RETURNS AND REFUNDS
=============================================================

4.1 Return Window
Standard return window: 10 days from delivery.
Electronics: 7 days.
Fashion: 30 days.

4.2 Seller Protection
Sellers are protected from returns due to buyer change of mind, 
provided the product was accurately described and correctly delivered.

=============================================================
5. APPEALS PROCESS
=============================================================

Account suspensions can be appealed via Seller Hub.
Appeal must include:
- Acknowledgment of issue
- Corrective action plan
- Supporting evidence (photos, invoices, tracking)

Review time: 5-10 business days.
