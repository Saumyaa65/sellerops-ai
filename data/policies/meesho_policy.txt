1. ACCOUNT HEALTH AND PERFORMANCE STANDARDS
=============================================================

1.1 Seller Rating
All sellers must maintain a minimum seller rating of 4.0 out of 5.0 at all times.
Seller ratings are calculated as the rolling 90-day average of customer feedback scores.

Rating thresholds and consequences:
- Rating 4.0 and above: Standard operating status
- Rating 3.5 to 3.9: Performance Improvement Notice issued. Seller must submit corrective action plan within 7 days.
- Rating 3.0 to 3.4: Elevated review. New listing approval may be paused. Existing listings may face reduced visibility.
- Rating below 3.0 for 30+ consecutive days: Account suspension proceedings initiated.

Ratings are influenced by: product quality, description accuracy, packaging quality, shipping speed, and customer service response time.

1.2 Return Rate Policy
The maximum acceptable return rate is 10% for all product categories.
Return rate is calculated as: (Seller-Responsible Returns / Total Delivered Orders) x 100

Return rate thresholds:
- 0% to 10%: Acceptable range. No action required.
- 10.1% to 15%: Monitoring status. Seller receives automated alert.
- 15.1% to 20%: Performance review. Category restrictions may apply.
- 20.1% to 25%: Listing suppression for high-return products. Mandatory corrective action required.
- Above 25% for 30+ days: Account suspension risk. Immediate investigation triggered.

Note: Only seller-responsible returns affect your return rate. Buyer-responsible returns (buyer changed mind, ordered by mistake) are excluded from calculation.

Seller-responsible return reasons that affect return rate:
- Product not as described
- Wrong product delivered
- Defective product
- Poor quality / manufacturing defect
- Size mismatch when size chart was not provided
- Damaged product (seller packaging issue)
- Missing accessories or components
- Incorrect color delivered

Buyer-responsible return reasons (do not affect return rate):
- Buyer changed mind
- Ordered by mistake
- Found better price elsewhere
- No longer needed
- Gift purchase cancellation

1.3 Order Cancellation Rate
Seller-initiated cancellation rate must remain below 2.5% of total orders in any 30-day period.

Consequences of high cancellation rate:
- 2.5% to 3.9%: Warning notice
- 4% to 5.9%: Deduction of ₹50 per cancelled order beyond threshold
- Above 6%: Temporary listing suspension

Acceptable reasons for seller-initiated cancellation:
- Genuine stock exhaustion (must be reflected in inventory immediately)
- Product found damaged before dispatch
- Logistics partner unavailable in buyer's pincode

Unacceptable reasons (may result in penalty):
- Price error (use Meesho repricing tool instead)
- Change of mind
- Inability to fulfill orders taken in advance

1.4 Late Shipment Rate
All orders must be dispatched within the seller's stated handling time (maximum 2 business days).
Late shipment rate must remain below 3%.

Penalties for late shipment:
- 3% to 5%: Warning. Seller's search ranking reduced.
- 5% to 8%: ₹25 per order penalty applied for orders shipped late.
- Above 8%: Account placed on probation. Bulk listings may be temporarily suppressed.

Force majeure exceptions apply for natural disasters, government-declared shutdowns, and verified courier disruptions.

2. LISTING QUALITY STANDARDS
=============================================================

2.1 Product Images
Each listing must contain a minimum of 4 high-quality product images:
- Main image: White or solid light background. Product centered. Minimum 1000x1000 pixels. No watermarks, logos, borders, or promotional text.
- Angle shots: Minimum 2 additional views showing different angles of the product.
- Detail shots: At least 1 close-up showing texture, material, or important product details.
- Lifestyle image (recommended): Product in use or in context.

Additional requirements:
- No promotional text, stickers, or badges overlaid on product images
- Images must not contain competitor branding
- All images must accurately represent the actual product being sold
- Image-to-product accuracy is monitored. Mismatches detected by AI vision analysis will trigger listing review.

Products with fewer than 3 images will be automatically suppressed until corrected.
Products with zero images cannot be published.

2.2 Product Title Guidelines
Product titles must:
- Be between 20 and 150 characters
- Accurately describe the product
- Include brand name (if applicable), product type, key attribute, and variant
- Not contain all capitals except for brand acronyms
- Not repeat keywords more than twice (keyword stuffing is prohibited)
- Not contain promotional phrases such as "Best Price", "Guaranteed", "No. 1 Seller"
- Not contain competitor names

2.3 Product Description Standards
Product descriptions must:
- Be a minimum of 100 words
- Accurately describe product features, specifications, materials, and dimensions
- Not contain misleading claims or exaggerated statements
- Not use medical or health claims without appropriate certifications
- Not contain HTML or special characters (except hyphens and bullet points)
- Include care instructions for textiles and consumables

Descriptions shorter than 50 words will receive automatic "Thin Content" warning.
Descriptions shorter than 100 words will be flagged for improvement.

2.4 Size Charts (Apparel, Footwear, and Accessories)
All clothing, footwear, and accessories listings must include a complete, accurate size chart.

Size charts must:
- Follow Indian standard sizing (S, M, L, XL, XXL for clothing; numeric UK sizes for footwear)
- Include measurements in centimeters (chest, waist, hip, length for clothing)
- Be updated if product sizing changes between batches
- Match the actual product being sold (verified via periodic quality audits)

Failure to include a size chart for applicable categories will:
- Result in automatic "Size Chart Missing" flag on listing
- Reduce listing visibility by 40% in search rankings
- Lead to all size-related returns being categorized as seller-responsible

2.5 Pricing Policy
- Selling price must not exceed the printed MRP (Maximum Retail Price)
- Selling price must be greater than ₹0
- Prices must be consistent across all marketplace channels (no artificial price inflation)
- Flash sales and discounts must be pre-approved via Meesho Campaign Manager
- Bundled product pricing must reflect fair composite value

Price violations:
- Price exceeding MRP: Immediate listing suppression. Must be corrected before reinstatement.
- Zero or negative price: Draft status enforced.
- Artificial price inflation (inflating MRP before discount): 30-day listing suspension and warning.

2.6 Prohibited and Restricted Products
The following products are strictly prohibited on Meesho:
- Counterfeit or fake branded products
- Products infringing intellectual property rights
- Adult content
- Weapons, explosives, or related accessories
- Drugs, controlled substances, or narcotics
- Products making unverified medical claims (e.g., guaranteed cure, weight loss)
- Government-regulated items without proper licenses
- Stolen goods or goods without proof of purchase

Health and wellness products require additional documentation:
- Ayurvedic products: AYUSH license number must be provided
- Dietary supplements: FSSAI license required
- Medical devices: CDSCO clearance certificate mandatory
- Weight loss or fat burning products: Must not make unverifiable claims

Any seller found listing prohibited products will face immediate account suspension.

3. PAYOUT AND SETTLEMENT POLICIES
=============================================================

3.1 Settlement Cycle
Meesho processes seller payouts on a weekly basis, every Thursday.
Settlements cover all orders marked as "Delivered" status for 7 or more days prior to the settlement cut-off date.

Payout timeline:
- Order delivered on Day 0
- Settlement eligible from Day 7
- Payout processed next Thursday
- Amount credited to registered bank account within 1-2 business days

3.2 Payout Deduction Categories
Standard deductions from gross sales include:

a) Platform Fees (Commission):
Category-wise commission rates:
- Apparel (clothing): 10-12%
- Footwear: 12%
- Electronics: 6-8%
- Home and Kitchen: 8-10%
- Health and Beauty: 10-15%
- Sports and Fitness: 8-10%

b) Return Shipping Charges:
- ₹30-80 per return depending on weight and zone
- Only charged for seller-responsible returns

c) Penalty Deductions:
- Late shipment penalty: ₹25 per affected order
- Cancellation penalty: ₹50 per cancelled order beyond threshold
- Policy violation penalty: ₹500-5,000 depending on severity

d) GST Deductions:
- GST is deducted on commission amounts only, not on full order value
- GST rates follow product HSN codes
- Fashion/apparel: 5% GST
- Electronics: 18% GST
- Food and health supplements: 5% or 12% GST

3.3 Disputing Payout Deductions
Sellers may dispute payout deductions within 30 days of settlement date.

Dispute process:
1. Navigate to Seller Panel > Payments > Dispute Center
2. Select the specific settlement cycle
3. Choose the type of dispute (incorrect deduction, missing payment, incorrect rate)
4. Upload supporting documents: order invoices, delivery confirmation, original product photos
5. Submit dispute form
6. Meesho settlement team will review within 7-15 business days
7. If dispute upheld, amount credited in next settlement cycle

Dispute eligibility:
- Disputes filed after 30 days of settlement will not be accepted
- Disputes without supporting documentation may be rejected
- Repeated frivolous disputes may result in dispute privilege suspension

3.4 Seller Tier Incentives
Meesho operates a tiered seller program with benefits:
- Bronze Tier (new sellers, <1000 orders): Standard commission rates
- Silver Tier (1000-5000 orders, rating 4.0+): 1% commission reduction, priority support
- Gold Tier (5000+ orders, rating 4.2+): 2% commission reduction, dedicated account manager, early access to campaigns
- Platinum Tier (invitation only, top 1% sellers): Maximum benefits, custom commission structure

4. RETURN HANDLING POLICY
=============================================================

4.1 Return Windows
Category-specific return windows from date of delivery:
- Clothing and Footwear: 7 days
- Electronics and Appliances: 10 days
- Home and Kitchen: 7 days
- Sports and Fitness: 7 days
- Health Products: Non-returnable (except defective)
- Perishables: Non-returnable

Extended return windows apply during special sale events (Meesho Maha Sale, Festival Sales).

4.2 Return Pickup Process
When a buyer initiates a return:
1. Seller receives return notification via email and Seller Panel
2. Seller has 24 hours to approve or escalate the return
3. If approved, courier is dispatched for pickup within 48 hours
4. Product undergoes quality check upon receipt at Meesho warehouse
5. If product passes QC, refund is processed to buyer
6. Return shipping cost charged to seller (if seller-responsible)

4.3 Appeal Process for Returns
Sellers may appeal returns categorized as seller-responsible within 14 days of return completion.

Appeal requirements:
- High-resolution photos of original product before dispatch
- Courier pickup proof showing correct product was collected
- Any other evidence establishing buyer fault
- Written explanation of why return should be reclassified

Appeals are reviewed within 10 business days. Maximum 3 appeals per month per seller account.

4.4 Quality Check Failure
If a returned product fails quality inspection:
- Product is quarantined and not returned to seller inventory
- Refund may still be processed to buyer depending on Meesho's judgment
- Seller may face additional penalty if product was demonstrably defective before dispatch

5. ACCOUNT SUSPENSION AND REINSTATEMENT
=============================================================

5.1 Grounds for Account Suspension
Immediate suspension (no warning):
- Selling counterfeit or IP-infringing products
- Fraudulent activity including fake order generation
- Providing false documents during seller onboarding
- Threatening or harassing buyers or Meesho staff
- Attempting to circumvent platform policies

Graduated suspension (after warnings):
- Seller rating below 3.0 for 30+ consecutive days
- Return rate above 25% for 30+ consecutive days
- Order cancellation rate above 8% for 30+ days
- Three or more policy violations within 6 months
- Non-compliance with mandatory listing standards after 2 warnings

5.2 Suspension Process
When an account is suspended:
- All active listings are immediately paused
- Pending payouts are held until suspension is resolved
- New order acceptance is disabled
- Seller Panel access is restricted to Appeals section only

5.3 Reinstatement Process
To appeal a suspension:
1. Access Seller Panel > Account > Appeals
2. Review the specific suspension reason
3. Prepare a comprehensive Appeal Package:
   a. Written acknowledgment of the violation
   b. Root cause analysis (what caused the violation)
   c. Corrective actions already taken
   d. Evidence of corrections (photos, documentation)
   e. Commitment to future compliance with timeline
4. Submit appeal (Meesho reviews within 5-7 business days)
5. If approved: Account reinstated with 90-day monitoring period
6. If rejected: Seller may submit revised appeal after 30 days

Appeal package quality directly impacts reinstatement success. Generic appeals without specific actions are typically rejected.

5.4 Monitoring Period Post-Reinstatement
Reinstated accounts are subject to 90-day enhanced monitoring:
- Weekly performance review
- Any new violation during monitoring period results in permanent account closure
- Seller must maintain all metrics above threshold during monitoring

6. SHIPPING AND LOGISTICS POLICY
=============================================================

6.1 Handling Time
- Standard handling time: 1-2 business days from order confirmation
- Extended handling time (special categories, custom orders): Maximum 3 business days
- Sellers must update handling time in Seller Panel if capabilities change

6.2 Approved Shipping Partners
Meesho has tie-ups with the following courier partners:
- Delhivery
- Blue Dart
- DTDC
- Ekart Logistics
- XpressBees
- Shadowfax

Sellers using non-approved couriers must provide tracking numbers and take full responsibility for delivery.

6.3 Packaging Standards
All packages must be:
- Securely sealed to prevent tampering
- Appropriately sized to prevent product damage
- Labeled with complete shipping label (provided by Meesho)
- Bubble-wrapped or foam-padded for fragile items
- Not reuse competitor packaging without covering branding

Meesho conducts random packaging audits. Poor packaging that leads to damage is seller's responsibility.

=============================================================
7. CUSTOMER COMMUNICATION POLICY
=============================================================

7.1 Response Time Requirements
- Sellers must respond to all customer queries within 24 hours
- During festival sales: 12-hour response time required
- Emergency safety complaints: 2-hour response mandatory

7.2 Communication Guidelines
Sellers must:
- Maintain professional and courteous communication at all times
- Provide accurate information about products and shipping
- Not request customers to cancel orders to avoid returns
- Not share personal contact information (phone, email, WhatsApp)
- Not offer transactions outside Meesho platform

Sellers must not:
- Use abusive, threatening, or discriminatory language
- Manipulate buyers with false urgency
- Offer bribes or incentives for positive reviews
- Contact buyers after order completion for marketing purposes

7.3 Review Solicitation Policy
Sellers may not:
- Offer discounts, free products, or cash in exchange for reviews
- Ask buyers to change negative reviews
- Use third-party review manipulation services
- Generate fake reviews through paid accounts

Violations of review policy result in immediate listing removal and potential account suspension.

=============================================================
8. INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY POLICY
=============================================================

8.1 Brand Authenticity
Sellers listing branded products must:
- Be authorized resellers of the brand
- Maintain authorization certificate
- Provide proof of purchase from authorized distributor
- Not modify original branded packaging

8.2 Copyright and Trademark
All product images, descriptions, and content must be original or properly licensed.
Sellers must not:
- Copy product descriptions from competitor listings
- Use brand logos without authorization
- List products under false brand claims

8.3 Counterfeit Policy
Selling counterfeit products is a zero-tolerance violation.
Any evidence of counterfeit products results in:
- Immediate account suspension
- Legal notice under applicable IP laws
- Permanent account ban (no appeal possible)

=============================================================
9. DATA AND PRIVACY POLICY
=============================================================

9.1 Buyer Data Protection
Sellers receive limited buyer information for order fulfillment only.
Sellers must not:
- Store buyer personal information beyond order completion
- Use buyer data for marketing
- Share buyer information with third parties
- Access buyer data outside of order fulfillment context

Violations of buyer data privacy may result in legal action under PDPB (Personal Data Protection Bill) India.
